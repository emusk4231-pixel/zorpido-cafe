Our Humble Beginning

Born from Vision. Building with Care.

was founded to create something better right here in Nepal — a place where good food is served with care and every visit feels special. Zorpido’s mission is to build the strongest ecosystem in Nepal’s food industry — one that empowers local people, delivers exceptional service, and keeps improving every single day.

Zorpido

The word Zorpido doesn’t have a specific meaning in any dictionary. It was created for the hospitality world to build somethings better in Nepal, a name built around passion, innovation, and care. Zorpido stands for doing things with speed, heart, and purpose serving good food fast, while embracing time, technology, and the human warmth that makes every visit special.
— Santosh
